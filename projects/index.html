  • The Guardian 👾 The Lone Denier†

    A custom clone of a Space Invaders game, as a light-hearted approach to climate change deniers

    My roleFront-end Developer

    Key technologies usedPixi.js, Grunt

    Most challenging challengeInteraction differences between mobile and desktop devices

    The solutionFor small screens, I decreased the number of 'invaders' (in comparison to the number on larger screens) whilst increasing the dimensions to improve visibility. I implemented additional on-screen controls for devices that lack keyboard support.

  • Feeding America 🌎 Hunger in America†

    An animated scrolling site following the stories of four people struggling with hunger

    My roleFront-end Developer

    Key technologies usedScrollMagic, GreenSock, Grunt

    Most challenging challengeHandling the sheer number of animations, timing and layering of assets

    The solutionI used a mixture of CSS position and translate to keep images hidden off-screen, until tweened into view. I divided GreenSock tweens into 'scenes' based on the storyboard provided, to keep the arrangement of animations managable.
